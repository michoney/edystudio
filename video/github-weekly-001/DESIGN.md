# GitHub Weekly Visual System

## Overview

Edy.Ai.Studio GitHub Weekly combines a dark technical grid with highly saturated editorial cards. The hierarchy is poster-like: a large rounded headline leads into a two-column ranking wall. Thick black outlines, offset shadows, pills, and oversized lowercase repository names create a playful technology-magazine identity.

## Colors

- **Canvas**: `#0B1115` — deep blue-black background.
- **Grid**: `#18292F` — technical graph-paper lines.
- **Ink**: `#080B0D` — outlines, shadows, and card text.
- **Paper**: `#E9EEF0` — headline and primary text.
- **Cyan**: `#53D8F3` — signal accents and cards.
- **Pink**: `#FF6C9F` — lead and Office cards.
- **Yellow**: `#FFD13B` — Skills card.
- **Purple**: `#AA82F4` — code graph and Orca cards.
- **Green**: `#68E69A` — LLM apps and Codex cards.
- **Orange**: `#FF9C4A` — trading card.

## Typography

- **Display**: Arial Rounded MT Bold, 700–900. Oversized title and lowercase repository names.
- **Chinese**: PingFang SC, 700–900. Explanations and CTA copy.
- **Data labels**: SFMono-Regular / monospace, 700–900. Rankings, stars, and weekly gains.

## Elevation

Cards use a 5px `#080B0D` outline and a 12–16px hard offset shadow. Depth is graphic rather than photorealistic. Cyan glows are reserved for the signal line and title pill.

## Components

- **Signal Pill**: dark rounded capsule with cyan technical label.
- **Poster Headline**: oversized white rounded lettering with a dark offset duplicate.
- **Ranking Cards**: saturated rounded rectangles with black outline, hard shadow, and translucent corner bubble.
- **Stat Pills**: white star capsule paired with a black weekly-growth capsule.
- **Technical Grid**: persistent graph-paper background with a moving scan line.

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do's
- Use hard outlines, offset shadows, and saturated card colors.
- Keep one dominant repository name per card.
- Use alternating left/right entrances and elastic settling.
- Maintain high contrast and mobile-first 9:16 framing.

### Don'ts
- Do not use glassmorphism or soft corporate gradients.
- Do not use realistic stock photography.
- Do not make all cards identical in motion or rotation.
- Do not place platform UI controls inside the video design.
