- Mood: technical anticipation, then playful impact.
- Camera: fixed 9:16 poster canvas.
- Motion: grid scan begins; signal pill drops in; white title slams upward with a small overshoot; subtitle follows.

## Beat 3 — Ranking wall (5.8–11.6s)

- Cards 05–10 continue to enter as the whole two-column wall scrolls upward.
- A cyan scan line and subtle grid drift maintain ambient activity.

## Beat 4 — Creator signature (11.6–14.0s)

- A dark ink panel wipes upward over the card wall.
- CTA lines enter with three different motions: vertical lift, scale pop, and tracking settle.
- Final 0.7 seconds hold the Edy.Ai.Studio signature for recognition.
